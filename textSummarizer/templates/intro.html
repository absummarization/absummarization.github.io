Welcome to Text Summarizer!


What is it?

Text Summarization is a task of generating a short and concise summary that captures the salient ideas of the source text.
This is a tool to address the ever-growing amount of text data available online to both better help discover most relevant information and to consume relevant information faster.

In general, there are two different approaches for automatic summarization: Abstractive summarization and Extractive summarization.
This model will present both results for your comparison.

How to use?

By simply inputting a website URL or plain text, you will get a one-sentence abstractive summary of what the content is about with the Google Pegasus pretrained model on XSUM dataset. An extractive summary will also be displayed.
